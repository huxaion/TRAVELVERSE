# Competitor Analysis

## What competitors do well
- Booking.com: booking breadth and conversion flow
- Airbnb: local-style stays and visual storytelling
- Tripadvisor: high-volume travel reviews
- Google Travel/Flights: flight intelligence and tracking
- Skyscanner/Hopper: price discovery and alerts
- Rome2Rio: multi-modal route clarity
- Wanderlog: itinerary collaboration and map planning

## Gaps TravelVerse addresses
- Unified social + booking + itinerary + safety in one system
- Hidden gems, scam alerts, and tourist-trap avoidance as first-class product layers
- Open-source destination intelligence contribution model
- Student-budget and female traveler safety modes
